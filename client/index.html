Natural Therapies in Central Scotland

We provide therapies for health and wellbeing, specialising in complex chronic illnesses such as Lyme disease. We use primarily Biomagnetic Pair Therapy (Medical Biomagnetism) with elements of Naturopathy and Biodecoding.

Biomagnetic pair therapy works at a physical, emotional and energetic level, and can be used for everything from infectious diseases to mental health issues. We offer naturopathic advice which involves everything from herbs to supplements, diet, lifestyle, environmental factors and any other natural advice which may be helpful. We also offer advice on emotional and psychological wellbeing, such as how to deal with suppressed emotions and trauma, negative belief systems and thought patterns.
