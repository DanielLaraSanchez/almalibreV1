Biomagnetic Pair

The Biomagnetic Pair (BMP) is a therapeutic technique developed by the Mexican doctor and scientist Dr. Isaac Goiz Durán. It involves the use of kinesthetic testing to detect imbalances in the body’s pH, and the application of magnets for therapeutic benefit and to return the body to homeostasis. Medical biomagnetism is a non-invasive, painless therapy, which improves recovery time without the side effects associated with conventional medicine.

What is it used for?

The Biomagnetic Pair technique is used worldwide as a complementary therapy for many ailments including chronic illnesses, infectious diseases, degenerative conditions, psycho-emotional and tumoral diseases, as well as poisonings. It is widely used in cases of Lyme disease, chronic fatigue, ME, fibromyalgia, cancer, eczema, psoriasis, arthritis, thyroid issues, IBS and other complex chronic illnesses.

The technique aims to eliminate the root causes of a disease, plus any physical or energetic imbalances that worsen it. The use of this therapy reduces clinical symptoms, rehabilitates different functions of the body, encourages homeostasis and reestablishes the person's health and wellbeing. The result in many cases is a clinical improvement in the patient’s condition, without experiencing the side effects of conventional treatments, or antimicrobial resistance, improving overall recovery time and rehabilitation.

How was it discovered?

Dr. Broeringmeyer, a Chief Medical Officer with NASA, noticed a difference in the right and left leg lengths when astronauts returned to Earth after missions into space. He theorised it was due to being away from the Earth's magnetic field, and discovered that the astronauts' legs returned to normal after the application of magnets to their bodies. Dr. Goiz later developed this as a form of therapy through many hours of investigation with his patients (primarily AIDS patients) after hearing Dr. Broeringmeyer speak at a session about Bioenergy, and went on to discover over 350 magnetic pairs, each relating to a different illness.

How is it perfomed?

Medical biomagnetism is a relaxing, non-invasive, painless therapy. Applied kinesiology (a muscle test) and a magnet is used to identify areas of pH imbalance. Your therapist will then apply magnets to the affected areas for 15 to 20 minutes. 3 sessions are recommended as minimum. Chronic or degenerative conditions may require multiple sessions. The frequency of sessions will vary for each client but weekly treatments followed by monthly sessions is not uncommon.

Ruth has trained in Medical Biomagnetism with Dr Isaac Goiz in Madrid and also undertaken a practical course in Ibiza, where she has previously worked as a therapist. She is able to combine her knowledge of Naturopathy, Biodecoding and other therapeutic training to provide complete holistic advice. The first session can take between 1.5 hrs to 2 hrs. Follow-up sessions 1 hr to 1.5 hrs.

How does BMP treatment work?

In the seminar which Dr Goiz attended, Dr. Broeringmeyer taught that organs and tissues in the body can become magnetically polarized, negatively or positively, and explained his technique to locate magnetic polarizations using applied kinesiology. Applied kinesiology is an alternative medicine method of diagnosis and treatment based on muscle strength testing. Its premise is that muscles have specific relationships with organs and glands and therefore weakness in a specific muscle is an indicator of a problem in the associated organ or gland. Proponents of applied kinesiology contend that healing that muscle weakness will also heal the problem in the associated organ or gland. Dr. Broeringmeyer found polarizations in the body by looking for changes in muscle strength. He then applied a magnet to the polarized area, to cause depolarization, thus returning the area to it's correct healthy magnetic charge.

What causes polarization within the body?

The therapy is based on the premise that the presence of pathogens is the reason for the polarisation of an area, as they require a certain altered pH for survival. There are also 'emotional' points where the area is said to have polarised due to emotional energy. Dr Goiz does not focus too much on why the area became polarised, knowing that since the affected areas can be located and corrected there is no need for further investigation.

However factors such as physical injury, emotional trauma and electromagnetic interference are suggested. It is also suggested that the pathogen can only be present in an area that is predisposed or already polarised, so the question is, what changes the pH before the pathogen sets up home? Emotional energy is becoming an ever-more discussed topic as playing a role in disease.

Therapies such as Biodecoding and Bioneuroemotion state that suppressed emotions from a long standing conflict or traumatic experience carry an electrical charge, this energy is thought to be able to polarise or weaken different areas of the body, depending on the nature of the conflict or emotion. Biomagnetic pair therapy would help to return the tissue back to a healthy pH, releasing the stuck emotional energy. There are even suggestions that certain pathogens resonate with certain emotions. This seems especially true in the case of Lyme disease, where the line between PTSD symptoms and Lyme symptoms is often blurred.

Biodecoding

The therapy technique Biodecoding, or Bioneuroemotion, works on the theory that physical symptoms are often an expression of a suppressed or unconscious emotion. Over time, the energy held by the emotion inside your body affects your physical health, creating symptoms that can be decoded. Changing a belief starts with releasing the emotion connected to the trauma. A physical symptom that has arisen, can in turn influence your feelings and emotions, which perpetuates the whole thing, and there are even theories about pathogens resonating with certain emotions. This further shows the need for a multi-layered approach to healing to find the root, release the emotion, retrain the thoughts and break the cycle. The theory of emotional energy causing physical symptoms has it's foundations in Dr Hamer's 'German New Medicine' and these ideas were later developed into therapeutic techniques by Enric Corbera (Bioneuroemotion) and Christian Fleche (Biodecoding). Naturopathy

Naturopathy is the use of various natural treatments and therapies to support the body in healing itself. Any illness is a two sided coin. A disease can only affect a body or organ that is somehow weakened, whether physically, or energetically. The body is the environment, that allows for the disease. Like a cake mould and the cake. Specific conditions in the environment allow for specific diseases to arise. Therefore healing is also a two sided coin, using treatments and techniques to heal the environment, and on the other hand, tackle the pathogens causing the disease. Naturopathy puts the emphasis on the former, reducing the need for the latter. It involves a holistic view of all the symptoms a person has, whether physical or psycho-emotional, and suggesting natural treatments to improve their all-round health and well-being. This may include advice on herbal medicine, supplements, external environmental factors, diet, lifestyle changes, massage, reflexology points, acupressure points, homeopathy, emotional release, examining core beliefs and thought patterns, exercise, breathing techniques, aromatherapy and so on. Though many of these factors may not be the cause of the current illness, they all contribute to how the body is going to heal. Herbal medicine and aromatherapy can work on both physical factors and emotional ones at the same time, and also affect your thoughts. Eg. One essential oil can be antiviral and anti-inflammatory, while also being calming, relaxing your nervous system and your body, interrupting a panic attack or a negative thought cycle. In Central America the metaphysical side of plant medicine is also taken into consideration, not just the chemical components. This multi-layered effect is possibly what makes herbal medicine work better for some people than modern 'allopathic' medicine, however modern medicine definitely has its place too. Naturopathy not only looks at alternative medicine, but also how the client has become unwell, and therefore the steps involved in allowing healing to now take place. Generally a little advice will be combined with the magnet therapy or focused on in a separate session for a more complete consultation.
